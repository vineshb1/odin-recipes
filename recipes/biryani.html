Biryani

Biryani is a south asian rice dish that can be made using various kinds of meats and spices.

Ingredients

  • 4 TBSP Vegetable Oil
  • 4 Small Potatoes
  • 2 Large Onions
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 TBSP Ginger Root
  • 2 Medium Tomatoes
  • 1 TSP Salt
  • 1 TSP Ground Cumin
  • 1/2 TSP Chili, Cardamom, Black Pepper, and Turmeric Powder
  • 2 TBSP Plain Yogurt
  • 1 Cinnamon Stick
  • 3LBS Boneless, Skinless Chicken Pieces

Steps

  1. Fry potatoes in 2 TBSP oil until lightly browned; set aside
  2. Cook onions, garlic, and ginger in another 2 TBSP of oil. when onion is golden, add tomatoes, salt, cumin, chili powder, pepper, and turmeric and stir constantly for 5 minutes
  3. Stir in yogurt, mint, cardamom, and cinnamon stick. Cover and cook on low heat, stirring occasionaly until tomatoes are pulp
  4. Add chicken and stir, then cover and cook on very low until chicken is tender
  5. Repeat Step 2. when done, stir it into the rice with heated stock and salt
  6. Add chicken mixture, stir, reduce heat to very low, cover and steam for 20 minutes
  7. Plate the dish

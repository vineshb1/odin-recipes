Lasangna

A plated lasagna

This lasagna recipe is quick, easy to make, and easy to clean up afterwards!

Ingredients

  • Meat of choice
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Product
  • Sugar
  • Fresh Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses of Choice
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Layer the lasagna
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. Let lasagna rest before serving
